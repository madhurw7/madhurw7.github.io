The Impossible List.

A plain old Bucket List is just not going to cut it. So, I took inspiration from Thomas Frank of College Info Geek.Put simply, the impossible list is an ever-evolving list of experiences that build upon each other, help others as well as yourself, and implore you to take action. This is mine, which is, of course, ever-evolving. This page is very personal and consists of everything that I've ever wanted to do.
